WELCOME TO STAY AND DINE HOTEL


Located in Rawalpindi, the enigmatic city of Pakistan which sits on the beautiful Pothohar region.Stay And Dine Hotel is perched along the main Stadium road in the heart of central Rawalpindi, with the capital city just a few kilometers away, you can have easy access to bustling shopping areas and calm environment of Islamabad from one place. It's a contrast that perfectly sums up our evolving city: ancient, modern and a little daring, all at once. Join us for a three course meal, followed by a relaxing treatment in our comfortable spacious rooms, then come discover twin cities with us.
